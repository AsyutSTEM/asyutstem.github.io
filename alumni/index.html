STEM is the best experience one could live in a high school. It gives its students the opportunity to grow up much faster and healthier; in an environment designed for those who want to change their micro-environment -or even the macro-environment- to the best. Overview

The first STEM high school and ranked as the best high school in Upper Egypt. With about 350 current student body from more than 15 governorates all over Egypt that makes great diversity among students, STEM Asyut is a coed, board, project-based high school that is located in a suburban setting near Assiut city.
